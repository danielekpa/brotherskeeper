About us


Brothers Keeper is a faith based organization that offers mental health services to it’s immediate community with the vision to teach and enlighten people on how deep mental illness has cut through our society.

Brothers Keeper also aims to provide support for people suffering from mental illnesses employing professional mental health techniques deeply rooted in the Christian faith.
